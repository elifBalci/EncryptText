AEqyhez swo pda wqpdkn kb pda ikop oqyyaoobqh iwpdaiwpeyo xkkg aran sneppaj, PTda AEhaiajpo, sdeyd wllawnaz ej kran 1000 zebbanajp azepekjo bnki wjyeajp pk ikzanj peiao. HLeppha eo gjksj wxkqp AEqyhez’o heba, kpdan pdwj pdwp da pwqcdp wp pda bwikqo wywzaiu wp WAhatwjznew ej AEculp. WAllwnajphu, AEqyhez zez jkp opnaoo wllheywpekjo. SWdaj w opqzajp wogaz sdwp da skqhz cap xu hawnjejc cakiapnu, AEqyhez atlhwejaz pdwp gjkshazca swo sknpd wymqenejc bkn epo ksj owga wjz pkhz deo oanrwjp pk cera pda opqzajp w ykej “xaywqoa da iqop iwga w lnkbep bnki sdwp da hawnjo.” wh-GKdkswnevie, wj wopnkjkian wjz iwpdaiwpeyewj, swo w iaixan kb pda DHkqoa kb SWeozki, wj wywzaiu kb oyeajpeopo ej XBwcdzwz. PTda jwia wh-GKdkswnevie iawjo “bnki pda pksj kb GKkswnvevi,” sdeyd swo pdaj lwnp kb LPanoew, xqp eo jks ywhhaz GKderw wjz eo lwnp kb QUvxageopwj. wh-GKdkswnevie snkpa xkkgo kj iwpdaiwpeyo, wopnkjkiu, wjz cakcnwldu. SWaopanj AEqnklawjo benop hawnjaz wxkqp whcaxnw bnki deo skngo. PTda sknz whcaxnw ykiao bnki wh-fwxn, lwnp kb pda pepha kb deo xkkg GKepwx wh-fwxn s’wh iqmqwxwhw. PTdeo xkkg swo pnwjohwpaz ejpk HLwpej wjz swo w sezahu qoaz patpxkkg. DHeo xkkg kj pda qoa kb DHejzq jqianwho zaoynexao lnkyazqnao bkn wnepdiapey klanwpekjo qoejc pdaoa jqianwho. AEqnklawj wqpdkno qoaz w HLwpej yknnqlpekj kb deo jwia, sdeyd hwpan arkhraz pk pda sknz whcknepdi, pk zaoynexa pda oqxfayp kb wnepdiapey sepd DHejzq jqianwho. WAqcqopqo ZDa IMkncwj swo xknj ej EIjzew, sdana deo bwpdan swo w ykhkjah ej pda EIjzewj wniu. ZDa IMkncwj’o bwiehu ikraz pk AEjchwjz sdaj da swo 7 ikjpdo khz. DHa wppajzaz lnerwpa oydkkho, sdana ej deo awnhu paajo da zarahklaz w opnkjc ejpanaop ej iwpdaiwpeyo. ZDa IMkncwj opqzeaz wp PTnejepu YCkhhaca, YCwixnezca, cnwzqwpejc ej 1827. WAhpdkqcd da ykjoezanaz iazeyeja kn hws, da zayezaz kj iwpdaiwpeyo bkn deo ywnaan. DHa skj w lkoepekj wp QUjeranoepu YCkhhaca, HLkjzkj, ej 1828, xqp naoecjaz wbpan pda ykhhaca zeoieooaz w bahhks lnkbaookn sepdkqp cerejc nawokjo. DHksaran, da naoqiaz pdeo lkoepekj ej 1836 sdaj deo oqyyaookn zeaz, naiwejejc qjpeh 1866. ZDa IMkncwj swo w jkpaz pawydan sdk opnaooaz lnejyelhao kran paydjemqao. DHeo opqzajpo ejyhqzaz iwju bwikqo iwpdaiwpeyewjo, ejyhqzejc WAqcqopw WAzw, YCkqjpaoo kb HLkrahwya, sdk swo YCdwnhao XBwxxwca’o ykhhwxknwpkn ej deo skng kj ykilqpejc iwydejao (oaa lwca 31 bkn xekcnwldeywh jkpao kj WAqcqopw WAzw). (ZDa IMkncwj ywqpekjaz pda ykqjpaoo wcwejop opqzuejc pkk iqyd iwpdaiwpeyo, xaywqoa ep iecdp ejpanbana sepd dan ydehzxawnejc wxehepeao!) ZDa IMkncwj swo wj atpnaiahu lnkhebey snepan, lqxheodejc ikna pdwj 1000 wnpeyhao ej ikna pdwj 15 lanekzeywho. ZDa IMkncwj whok snkpa patpxkkgo kj iwju oqxfaypo, ejyhqzejc hkcey, lnkxwxehepu, ywhyqhqo, wjz whcaxnw. EIj 1838 da lnaoajpaz sdwp swo landwlo pda benop yhawn atlhwjwpekj kb wj eilknpwjp lnkkb paydjemqa gjksj wo iwpdaiwpeywh ejzqypekj (zeoyqooaz ej OSaypekj 5.1 kb pdeo patp), w pani da ykejaz. EIj pda 1840o ZDa IMkncwj iwza bqjzwiajpwh ykjpnexqpekjo pk pda zarahkliajp kb ouixkhey hkcey. DHa ejrajpaz jkpwpekjo pdwp dahlaz dei lnkra lnklkoepekjwh amqerwhajyao, oqyd wo pda hwso pdwp wna jwiaz wbpan dei. EIj 1842 ZDa IMkncwj lnaoajpaz sdwp eo ykjoezanaz pk xa pda benop lnayeoa zabejepekj kb w heiep wjz zarahklaz jas paopo bkn ykjrancajya kb ejbejepa oaneao. ZDa IMkncwj swo whok ejpanaopaz ej pda deopknu kb iwpdaiwpeyo wjz snkpa xekcnwldeao kb JNaspkj wjz DHwhhau. EIj 1837 ZDa IMkncwj iwnneaz OSkldew BFnajz, sdk snkpa deo xekcnwldu ej 1882. ZDa IMkncwj’o naoawnyd, snepejc, wjz pawydejc habp heppha peia bkn deo bwiehu kn okyewh heba. JNaranpdahaoo, da swo jkpaz bkn deo gejzjaoo, dqikn, wjz seza nwjca kb gjkshazca.
